Chest Measurement

×

How to measure your chest:
1.Wrap the tape measure under your armpits, around the fullest part of your chest.
2.The tape measure should be snug. Not so tight that it constricts breathing, but not so loose that the tape measure slides down.
3.Don't puff out or flex your chest. Just stand normally.

Waist Measurement

×

How to measure your waist:
1.Start at the top of your hip bone, then bring the tape measure all the way around your body, level with your belly button.
2.Make sure it's not too tight and that it's straight, even at the back. Don't hold your breath while measuring.
3.Check the number on the tape measure right after you exhale.

Hip Measurement

×

How to measure your hips:
To measure your hips, remove your outer garments, put your feet together, and wrap a soft measuring tape straight and snug around the widest part of your hips. Your hip measurement is the point at which the end of the tape meets the remaining length.

Sleeve Length Measurement
